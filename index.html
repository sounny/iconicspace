Research Context

About This Archive

Purpose

IconicSpace is a scholarly research archive for studying visual culture in the commercial space industry. All materials are presented for academic study, commentary, and criticism. Non-commercial.

Citation

Sounny-Slitine, S. and Bensalem, S. (forthcoming) "Branding the Void: Visual Identity, Institutional Isomorphism, and Placemaking in the Commercial Space Industry," Space Policy.

Methodology

200 organizations coded across four dimensions: typography, iconography, color, and composition (Krippendorff, 2004; Kress & van Leeuwen, 2006; Henderson & Cote, 1998).

Framework

Two lenses: institutional isomorphism (DiMaggio & Powell, 1983) explains convergence; visual placemaking (our concept) explains the cultural work logos perform in placeless environments.
